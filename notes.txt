Social auth providers:
- Discord (https://discord.com/developers/applications)
- Github (https://github.com/settings/developers)
- Twitter (https://developer.x.com/en/portal/projects-and-apps) Note: Use consumer keys
- Google (https://medium.com/@mimranisrar6/how-to-add-a-google-login-using-socialite-in-laravel-21f6eebafcec)

What we collect from socially authenticated user's public profile:
    - id
    - name
    - email
    - avatar image url
